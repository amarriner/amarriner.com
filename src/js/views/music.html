Early Years

Ages ago (or what seems like it, at any rate) I used to like making simple electronic music with tracker software. This is in the early 90’s, mind you. I still have much of that stuff I created, but the quality is super low since I recorded it as it was playing out of computer speakers into a tape deck (!). I posted a few of them up on my Bandcamp page. An example:

Caustic

More recently I’ve been playing with an Android app called Caustic (which has also been ported to PC). It’s a nice synthesizer/sequencer. Especially for an Android app. I’ve done a few really small things with it, but would love to do some more.
